# ONDC:FIS12 2.2.1 — Overview

## Summary
ONDC:FIS12 is the purchase finance domain for lending against consumer purchases. It enables a consumer to buy goods on loan and repay the amount in EMIs, including flows with and without Account Aggregator support.

## Sector & Purpose
Sector / industry: purchased goods loans.  
This domain solves the problem of letting consumers purchase goods on loans, so the purchase can be financed at the point of sale instead of paid fully upfront.

## Real-World Actors
The real-world transaction is simple: a consumer purchases something on EMI and avails a loan on that purchase. In practice, this involves the consumer, the seller of the goods, and the finance provider that extends the purchase loan.

## Use Cases
- PURCHASE FINANCE
- Purchase finance with Account Aggregator support
- Purchase finance without Account Aggregator support
- Purchase finance cancellation handling
- Loan foreclosure
- Missed EMI payment handling
- Pre-part payment handling
- Multiple offer handling

## Key Concepts
- Purchase finance
- Goods finance
- EMI
- Purchase goods on loan
- Account Aggregator

## Example Scenario
A consumer purchases an air conditioner and avails a loan on that via credit card or finance, then pays EMI. This is a typical purchase finance case where the buyer gets the product first and repays the financed amount over time.
